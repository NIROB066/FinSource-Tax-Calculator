    ①Basic = 60% · HRA = 30% · Medical = 6% · Conveyance = 4% of Gross

    ③PF: Employee 6% of basic + Office 6% of basic (employee share → rebate eligible)
    ④Rebate = 10% of admissible investment (updated from 15%)
    ⑤Admissible = min(3% of taxable income, actual×10%, ৳7,50,000)
    ⑥NEW: File Jul–Sep → 5% rebate on net tax (max ৳25,000)
    ⑦NEW: File Jan–Mar → +2% surcharge (min ৳3,000)
    ⑧NEW: File Apr–Jun → +5% surcharge (min ৳5,000)
