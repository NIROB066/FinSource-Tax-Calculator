Office Paid Tax Slab (Tax on Basic)

How your Office Tax works: Your employer automatically assumes you will make the maximum possible investments to get the highest rebate. Therefore, the Office will pay the lesser of (1) Tax computed purely on your Basic Salary, or (2) Your Assumed Net Tax if you got the maximum rebate. This ensures the company never pays more than your theoretical minimum tax liability.

    ①Basic = 60% · HRA = 30% · Medical = 6% · Conveyance = 4% of Gross
    ②Allowance exemption = min(৳5,00,000, 1/3 of Total Income)
    ③PF: Employee 6% of basic + Office 6% of basic (both shares → rebate eligible & added to income)
    ④Rebate = 10% of admissible investment
    ⑤Max Rebate = min(3% of taxable income, ৳7,50,000)
    ⑥OFFICE TDS: Your employer assumes you'll max out your investment rebate. Office pays min(Tax on Basic, Net Tax assuming max rebate).
